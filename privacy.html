Privacy Policy

Effective date: April 11, 2026

Zentsu LLC ("we," "us," or "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how our applications and services (collectively, the "App") handle your information. Please read this policy carefully before using the App.

1. Information We Collect

We collect only the minimum information necessary to provide core App functionality. This may include:

  • Information you provide directly — data you enter into the App such as notes, logs, settings, or preferences.
  • Device information — basic technical data such as device model, operating system version, and app version, used solely for troubleshooting and compatibility purposes.
  • Usage data — anonymized, aggregate information about how features are used (e.g., which screens are visited), to help us improve the App. This data does not identify you personally.

We do not collect your name, email address, location, or any other personally identifiable information unless you voluntarily provide it when contacting us for support.

2. How Your Data Is Stored

All personal data you enter into the App — including any health-related information — is stored on your device only. We do not transmit this data to our servers or any third-party servers. You remain in full control of your data at all times. If you delete the App, all locally stored data is removed from your device.

If the App offers optional cloud backup features (such as iCloud), your data may be stored in your personal cloud account, which is governed by that service's own privacy policy. We do not have access to data stored in your personal cloud accounts.

3. How We Use Your Information

Any information we collect is used solely to:

  • Operate and improve the App's features and performance.
  • Diagnose and fix technical issues.
  • Respond to support requests you initiate by contacting us.

We do not use your information for advertising or marketing purposes.

4. Sharing of Information

We do not sell, rent, trade, or otherwise share your personal information or health data with any third parties, including advertisers, data brokers, or analytics companies.

We may disclose information only if required to do so by law, regulation, court order, or other valid legal process, and only to the extent required.

5. Children's Privacy

Our App is not directed to children under the age of 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child under 13 has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately and we will take steps to remove that information.

6. Data Retention

Because data is stored on your device, you control retention entirely. You may delete your data at any time by clearing App data within the App settings or by uninstalling the App. If you contact us for support and share information in that communication, we retain that communication only as long as necessary to resolve your inquiry.

7. Security

We follow industry-standard practices to protect any data processed by the App. However, no method of electronic storage or transmission is 100% secure. We encourage you to protect your device with a strong passcode and to keep your operating system updated.

8. Your Rights

Because we do not maintain a database of your personal information, most personal data rights (access, correction, deletion) are exercised directly on your device. If you have questions about data we may hold from a support inquiry, please contact us at the address below and we will assist you promptly.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will revise the effective date at the top of this page. We encourage you to review this policy periodically. Your continued use of the App after any changes constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.
