A native Mac Lorem Ipsum generator. Set a word, sentence, or paragraph count and copy placeholder text in one click. No browser tab required.

What it does

Bench's Lorem Ipsum tool generates classic placeholder text at whatever granularity you need: a handful of words to fill a label, a sentence or two for a form hint, or several paragraphs for a layout under construction. Adjust the count with a stepper, choose your unit (words, sentences, paragraphs), and hit Copy. The text is on your clipboard immediately, with no ads or rate limits in the way and no Latin to know.

Why use Bench's Lorem Ipsum generator

Web generators work, but they involve opening a browser, finding the site again, and copying out of a textbox. When you're toggling between a design tool and a code editor all day, that round-trip adds up. Bench lives in a single macOS window: open it from the sidebar, set your count, and copy. It takes about three seconds and never interrupts your flow with a page reload or an ad placement.
