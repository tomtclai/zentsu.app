Legal

Privacy Policy

Effective date: July 13, 2026

This privacy policy applies to Bench (the "Application"), published by Zentsu LLC, and the Bench product-notes form on zentsu.app. Zentsu LLC is referred to below as "we," "us," or the "Service Provider."

What information does the Application obtain and how is it used?

Bench processes text, files, and images you provide entirely on your Mac. Its tools do not send that content to Zentsu. Registration is not required, and Bench has no Zentsu account system, analytics, advertising, or telemetry.

Does the Application collect precise real time location information of the device?

The Application does not collect precise information about the location of your device.

Do third parties see and/or have access to information obtained by the Application?

The Application does not transmit tool content to Zentsu, so Zentsu has no such content to share. Apple processes the App Store download, optional Bench Pro purchase, and purchase restoration. Zentsu does not receive your payment-card details from those transactions.

What information does the website collect?

The website does not use analytics, advertising cookies, tracking pixels, or behavioral profiling. Our hosting provider processes standard request information, such as your IP address and browser details, to deliver and secure the website.

If you subscribe to Bench product notes, the signup form sends your email address to Buttondown, our email service provider. Buttondown also records the IP address and referrer metadata associated with the signup. We use the subscriber record only to send occasional notes about Bench. We do not enable open tracking or link tracking in these emails, and we do not sell subscriber information.

How long is newsletter information kept?

Buttondown stores the subscriber record on our behalf. We keep your subscription until you unsubscribe, ask us to delete it, or we discontinue Bench product notes. You can unsubscribe from any email or ask us to delete your record by emailing privacy@zentsu.app. Buttondown may retain limited information where required by law or in isolated backups until those backups are deleted under its retention process.

Who processes newsletter information?

Buttondown LLC processes newsletter information for Zentsu LLC to manage subscriptions and send email. Its practices are described in Buttondown's privacy policy. You may request access, correction, or deletion of the newsletter information we control by contacting privacy@zentsu.app.

What are my opt-out rights?

You can stop using the Application by uninstalling it through the standard process on your device or application marketplace. Newsletter emails include an unsubscribe link, and you may also contact us to request deletion.

Children

The Application is not used to knowingly solicit data from or market to children under the age of 13.

The Service Provider does not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children. The Service Provider encourages all children to never submit any personally identifiable information through the Applications and/or Services. The Service Provider encourages parents and legal guardians to monitor their children's Internet usage and to help enforce this Policy by instructing their children never to provide personally identifiable information through the Applications and/or Services without their permission. If you have reason to believe that a child has provided personally identifiable information to the Service Provider through the Applications and/or Services, please contact the Service Provider at privacy@zentsu.app so that they will be able to take the necessary actions. You must also be at least 16 years of age to consent to the processing of your personally identifiable information in your country (in some countries we may allow your parent or guardian to do so on your behalf).

Security

The Application does not send your tool content to Zentsu LLC. Website and product-notes information is limited to the processing described above. No internet transmission or storage system can be guaranteed completely secure.

Changes

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time for any reason. The Service Provider will notify you of any changes to their Privacy Policy by updating this page with the new Privacy Policy. You are advised to consult this Privacy Policy regularly for any changes, as continued use is deemed approval of all changes.

Your choices

Submitting the product-notes form asks us to send the updates described beside that form and permits the related processing described above. You can withdraw that request at any time by unsubscribing or contacting us. Withdrawal does not affect processing completed before your request.

Contact Us

If you have any questions regarding privacy while using our Applications, or have questions about our practices, please contact the Service Provider via email at privacy@zentsu.app.
