Coil

Track every subscription in one place.

Coil is a native subscription tracker for iPhone and Mac. It runs on your device, never links to your bank, and needs no account. You add what you pay for, and Coil keeps the renewals, totals, and reminders in front of you. It's in beta now, with the App Store release close behind.

iOS · macOS · native Swift / SwiftUI{% if coil_price %} · ${{ coil_price }} one-time{% endif %}

What it does

  • Everything in one place

    Add each subscription once. Coil keeps the price, the billing cycle, and the next renewal date together, so you always know what's coming.

  • Renewal reminders

    A heads-up before a charge lands, so a free trial or a plan you meant to cancel doesn't quietly renew.

  • Private by design

    No bank linking and no account. What you enter stays on your devices, synced through your own iCloud. We run no server and receive nothing.

  • Native on every device

    Built in Swift and SwiftUI for iPhone and Mac, not a web view in a wrapper.

Availability

Coil is in private beta. It isn't on the App Store yet, so there's nothing to download from this page today. For how Coil handles your data, see the Coil privacy policy. For anything else, email hello@zentsu.app.
