Coil

Track every subscription in one place.

Coil is a native subscription tracker for iPhone and Mac. It runs on your device, never links to your bank, and needs no account. You add what you pay for, and Coil keeps the renewals, totals, and reminders in front of you. It's in TestFlight now, with the App Store release close behind.

iOS · macOS · native Swift / SwiftUI

What it does

  • Fast entry

    The built-in catalog covers 362 services and fills in the details when you pick one. Anything else takes a few fields. The catalog is bundled with the app and works offline.

  • Renewal calendar

    A month view of every upcoming charge, with an upcoming-payments list beside it, so you can see what lands and when.

  • Trials and cancel-by dates

    Record a trial's end date and the last day to cancel. A forgotten trial stays visible before it turns into a charge.

  • Real multi-currency

    Track subscriptions in different currencies. Coil converts the totals to your base currency using bundled exchange rates, so nothing phones home for a rate.

  • Spending insights

    Monthly and yearly totals, broken down by category.

  • Widgets

    Upcoming payments on the home screen, the lock screen, and the Mac desktop.

  • Full backup, free for everyone

    Complete JSON backup and restore is included at no cost. Your data stays portable and yours to move.

  • Private by design

    There is no account and no bank linking. What you enter stays on your devices and syncs through your own iCloud. We run no server and receive nothing. Skip iCloud and it all stays on the one device.

The tradeoff

Coil never connects to your bank, so it can't auto-detect what you pay for. You add subscriptions yourself. With the catalog, most people are done in a few minutes, and in exchange nothing about your money leaves your devices.
