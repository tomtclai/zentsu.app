Dial Pro · Estimated level

A clearer view of the doses you logged.

Dial plots an estimated medication level over time from your recorded doses and the half-life value shown in the app. Change the range to review a week, month, or six months of your own history.

This is an informational estimate, not a measured blood level, dose recommendation, or treatment instruction. Dial explains the method inside the app.

Quick logging

Keep the details with the dose.

Record medication, amount, date, time, injection site, and notes in a focused form. Your recent values make the next entry faster, while every field remains yours to confirm.

  • Edit an entry when something changes
  • Keep injection sites attached to each dose
  • Keep weight entries alongside the dose timeline

Dial Pro · PDF report

Bring a useful record to the conversation.

Create a PDF summary of dates, doses, weight entries, and applicable history already in Dial. Review it first, then share it with your prescriber when it helps.

The report summarizes your entries. It is not a medical record or treatment plan.

Privacy

On your device, with iCloud when you choose.

Dial keeps medication, dose, reminder, and supply records on your device. If you turn on the in-app iCloud control, those records can also use the private CloudKit database attached to your Apple Account. Zentsu runs no server that can read them.

Weight entries use a separate local store. Apple Health access is optional and only covers body weight when you grant permission.
