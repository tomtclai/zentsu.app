Dial Pro · Estimated level

A clearer view of the doses you logged.

Dial plots an estimated medication level over time from your recorded doses and the half-life value shown in the app. Change the range to review a week, month, or six months of your own history.

An estimate, and we say so. The chart models a standard decay curve from the doses you logged. It is built to help you notice your own patterns between doses, not to stand in for a measured blood level or suggest what to take next. Dial does not calculate doses. The app explains the method in full.

Quick logging

Keep the details with the dose.

Record medication, amount, date, time, injection site, and notes in a focused form. Your recent values make the next entry faster, while every field remains yours to confirm.

  • Edit an entry when something changes
  • Keep injection sites attached to each dose
  • Keep weight entries alongside the dose timeline

Dial Pro · History and trends

Review the pattern across your record.

Move between dose history, weight changes, and trends from side-effect entries already present in your record.

Dial Pro · PDF report

Bring the whole picture to your next visit.

Generate a PDF with your dose titration, injection sites, weight trend, and side-effect patterns. Dial builds it on your device, and you share it only when you decide to, by AirDrop, email, or print.

Private the rest of the time, and useful the one moment your record needs to leave your phone.

The report summarizes your entries. It is not a medical record or treatment plan.

Privacy

Clear about what stays on your device.

Accounts
There is no Dial account. Open the app and start logging.
Analytics
Dial runs no usage analytics or telemetry. We measure nothing about how you use it.
Our servers
Zentsu operates no server that stores or reads your records.
Optional sync
Turn on iCloud in the app and records sync through the private CloudKit database on your own Apple Account, not to us.

Medication, dose, reminder, and supply records stay on your device. Weight entries use a separate local store, and Apple Health access is optional, covering body weight only when you grant permission.
