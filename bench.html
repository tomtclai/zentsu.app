02 — METHOD

Smart where it counts. Quiet everywhere else.

A small rule-based check reads your clipboard and picks a tool. It runs on your Mac. Nothing is sent anywhere.

Smart Paste

Paste a JWT. The decoder's already open.

Copy a chunk of JSON, switch to Bench, and the formatter is waiting. Copy a hex color, and the converter is open. Copy a JWT, and it's already decoded. Nothing to click.

The detection is regex and string checks — maybe 200 lines. It runs on your Mac, it has no network code compiled in, and if it doesn't recognise what you pasted it just gives up quietly and lets you pick.
