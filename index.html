How we build

  • Native first

    Mac and iOS apps written in Swift and SwiftUI, not cross-platform shells wrapped around a web view.

  • Offline by default

    Tools that work without an account, without telemetry, and without a server round-trip. What you type stays on your device.

  • Pay once

    One-time purchases over subscriptions, where the model fits. The version you bought keeps working.

  • Quiet software

    We ship and iterate. We don't market with hype, and we don't add features we can't stand behind.
